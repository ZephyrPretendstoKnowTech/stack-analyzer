## What it does

- Build unlimited stacks with fully customizable line items
- Toggle costs between **per-user** and **flat monthly**
- Set your sell price via margin slider or exact price entry
- Add flat monthly customer add-ons on top of per-user pricing
- See Monthly Revenue, Annual Revenue, and Gross Profit per stack
- **Side-by-side comparison** showing which stack earns you more GP
- **Export to PDF** for QBRs and proposals
- Auto-saves progress in your browser
